Redesigning Mindfi Application

MindFi is a digital wellness application that helps users to improve mindfulness and gives users a meditation experience for modern working and living.

View Final Prototype

Problem

COVID-19 pandemic has had a significant impact on people's mental well-being. According to the COVID-19 Mental Wellness Task Force report, "Based on an Institute of Mental Health (IMH) study conducted during the pandemic, 8.7% of the surveyed Singapore population met the criteria for clinical depression, while 9.4% met the criteria for anxiety. Additionally, 9.3% met the criteria for mild to severe stress." Since the onset of the national Circuit Breaker in 2020, there has been a noticeable rise in the volume of calls received by IMH's Mental Health Helpline. Generally, mental health has always been a topic that is often overlooked.

Maintaining and enhancing mental health solutions after the COVID-19 pandemic is crucial, as mental well-being remains a vital component of overall health people can benefit from it. By continuing to support these solutions, we can assist individuals dealing with diverse stressors and challenges in their lives. Moreover, the lessons learnt and innovation made in developing mental health responses during the pandemic can be utilised to meet mental health needs in various other situations and crises.

Target Audience

Target audiences are people who might be going through something and the app can assist them to heal themselves, such as childhood trauma, anxiety, stress. The app consists of features that enables them to seek help with people of similar situations and professional help. While also keeping track of their own feelings and thoughts by journaling down.

Enhancement features

Encouraging notifications.

Encouraging notifications that pop up on the user’s screen to encourage and care for them. The app may even ask how the users are doing, if they haven’t been online. This is to encourage the users to stay online.



Journal

Journaling can help to prioritise problems, fears, and concerns, as well as track any symptoms on a daily basis to identify triggers and learn how to manage them more effectively. It also provides a space for positive self-talk and allows us to recognize and address negative thoughts and behaviours. This is something that people can use on the app at any time they want, even after covid.



Booking Therapy

Collaborating with therapists. Book a session, conduct therapy in person or virtually on the app, with booking, facetime and calling features. This is something that people can use on the app at any time they want, even after covid.



Direct Messages

Sometimes people need listening ears. They might want to look for people to talk to directly. Maybe even someone they don’t know in person.
