Redesigning Love, Bonito E-commerce Website

This project focuses on enhancing the Love, Bonito website with improved usability and new features to attract more traffic and engagement.

View Final Prototype

Enhancements

Favourite Option

Adding a "Favorite" option directly on the product display allows users to easily save items to their favorites.

Labelling Best Sellers and ratings

Labeling "Best Sellers" helps users identify the most popular products, while ratings provide insights into the best and most well-loved items.

Sold-out UI

When a product is completely sold out, its image will appear in black and white to indicate unavailability.

Digital Try-On!

We've all had moments

when we want to buy an outfit online but struggle to tell if it will suit us. That's why we have a smart solution—an AI-powered virtual try-on feature. Simply upload your photo, and the AI will generate an image of how you would look in the outfit, making online shopping more convenient and confident.
