Piggy Vault, Financial Web App

This web app is for helping people to manage their finances and saving money.

View Final Prototype

Web-App Description

Users can manage their spendings and savings. They will be rewarded if they consistently update their money details and stay active. A mini gamification part is added into the website to keep Users hook onto managing their finances. When users earn achievement/login daily, they gain piggy credits that can be used to gacha for piggy's. Users can show off how great of an finances expert they are through climbing the top spot in the ranking system, based on the amount of experience point by staying active and completing achievements.

User Stories

• As a designer, I prefer the website to be visually appealing so that I can attract people to use the website.

• As a user, I want to easily create and customize multiple budgets to effectively manage my income and expenses.

• As a user, I want a user-friendly interface that allows me to quickly input and categorize transactions, ensuring accurate tracking of my spending habits.

• As a user, I want the app to provide insightful financial reports and visualizations, helping me analyze my financial trends over time.

• As a socializer, I want to participate in a forum to share and learn money-saving tips, connecting with a community that supports my financial goals.
