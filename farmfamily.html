Farm Family, a family bonding app.

This app is about bringing family closer together.

View Final Prototype

Description

This app focuses on helping family bond and bringing them closer together. It consists of gamification to achieve the purpose of the app. There are also features like calender, which allows every family members to put down their availability, to better plan for future outings.

People might be willing to play

Socializer:

The app is desgined for users to foster connections by socialising and sharing posts with family members, in the family diary section. Users can also actively participate in conversation with random people online. The app features a calendar tool to facilitate coordination among family members for planning hangouts and checking family members availibility. In addition, there is a voting system for family to vote the plan they want. Besides the above functions, the app itself have mini challenging games that family members can play together. Every family members have to be online/ active to play the game, this is to promote family bonding, and spending time together. In the game itself, they have to communicate, team up and strategically come up with idea to beat the game. This part involves a lot of communicating. The design will attract users who love interacting and connecting with other users.

Free Spirit:

Another engaged player type is the “ free spirit”, motivated by the pursuit of rewards. The app has cosmetic rewards for their character and user icon wallpaper. The cosmetic can be exchange with active points. These users find joy in collecting these items simply for the sake of collecting, which add an element of personal enjoyment to their experience.

People might not be willing to play

Self Seeker:

On the flip side, users who are less inclined to actively participate fall into “self seeker”. These are people who help other users mainly for points, recognition or other gain. To appeal to these people the app has ranking system for family members using the app. This is ranked based on their active points, active points can be earned by completing achievement, daily login and engaging in the game content. Self seekers seek individual achievements and they may take pride in their family top-ranking status, which pushes them to collaborated with their family members to farm for active points. Eg. in the game, players have to work together to get pass different stages to earn active points, in a way, fight for the top spot.

Networkers:

“Networkers” are people who want to form social connections. To appeal to these group of people there is a public dairy section, where users can post their post to the public and communicate with random people online. They may inquire about family hangout spots or seek advice on personal matters, aligning with their preference for networking opportunities over extensive engagement with app features.

Maslow’s hierarachy of needs:

Physiological Needs:

Users engage in daily farming activities and challenges, providing a sense of achievement and competence, fulfilling the basic need for accomplishment.

Safety Needs:

The collaborative nature of the game, where family members work together on challenges and strive for higher rankings, creates a sense of security and belonging within the virtual game.

Social Needs:

Features like the social posts and collaborative game challanges contribute to fulfilling the need for social connection and communication. Users can plan hangouts and share experiences others, and engage in virtual socialization in the public diary section.

Esteem Needs:

Users can achieve higher rankings among the other families , earning rewards and unlocking cosmetic items. At the end of each game there is also a mini ranking system that show who is the mvp of that match, within the family. This recognition and sense of accomplishment satisfy the need for esteem and status within the game environment.
