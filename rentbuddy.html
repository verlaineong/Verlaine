Rent Buddy, Dog rental.

This web app is a fictional company that offers Dog Renting service. E.g. Walking dogs.

Description

Some people may want to walk experience the joy of spending time with dogs if they don't have one. Rent Buddy offers the service for people to experience it. They could also take it as a chance to experience how it's like to own a pet before deciding on getting their own pet. These dogs are like shelter dogs who might be abandoned by their owners.
